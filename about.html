Our Story

Xportesia was establiished in 2010. It started by only exportation of Yam to other West African countries with a total of 23 workers.

In 2013, it started the exportation of Potatoes, water melon and cassava. Today, xportesia is one of the biggest exportation companies in Nigeria. It exports more that 100 farm products and 50 other products with a total of 577 workers.
