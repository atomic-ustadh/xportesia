What is Tajweed التجويد?

Tajweed Literally means Proficiency of doing something well.

Tajweed can be defined Technically as the subject that deals with the rules and regulations governing the pronunciation of the letters and words of the Glorious Qur'an.

Tajweed consists of 2 aspects;

  1. The theoritical aspect (in arabic): It is mandatory on some muslims (in arabic)
  2. The practical aspect (in arabic): It is mandatory on everybody (in arabic) irrespective of who you are.
